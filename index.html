Frequently Asked Questions

Webots is a professional robot simulation software that enables the development, testing, and validation of autonomous systems in a virtual environment.

Our project leverages the advanced capabilities of Webots to simulate real-world driving scenarios, allowing us to test and refine our autonomous driving algorithms in a safe and controlled environment before deploying them in actual vehicles.

Our project stands out due to its focus on implementing cutting-edge algorithms and techniques for autonomous navigation and decision-making. We prioritize innovation, efficiency, and safety in our development process, aiming to push the boundaries of what's possible in autonomous driving technology.

While our primary focus is on showcasing the capabilities of our autonomous driving system, we're exploring ways to provide interactive elements for users to experience and understand the underlying technology better.

We welcome collaboration and engagement from individuals and organizations interested in autonomous driving technology. Feel free to reach out to us through our website or social media channels to learn more about our project.

The potential applications of our autonomous driving technology are vast, ranging from personal transportation and logistics to urban planning and smart city initiatives.

Safety is our top priority. We employ a multi-layered approach, including rigorous testing, simulation, and validation procedures to ensure the reliability and robustness of our autonomous driving algorithms

Our autonomous driving system integrates a variety of sensors, including LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging), radar, cameras, and ultrasonic sensors. These sensors work together to provide comprehensive environmental perception, enabling our vehicle to accurately detect and interpret its surroundings in real-time.
